End of a Century

Posted on 2018-12-24 |

It would be a bit too empty to think of nothingness on bed at 2AM of Christmas eve. No better time to write a blog that has been dead for 3 months.
A lot has happened in 2018, as any other year. It is a lot to look back now, but nothing when looking back later. How much can I say I have changed for the better in 10 years? But at the same time, perhaps I should be happy that I did not regress. Do you recall writing those cliche essay topics on, what is success and sorts? Well, as much as I can tell you, I have yet to be able to answer this question. One is never as successful as one wants to be, and successful can only be used to describe people not oneself. I could describe my year as a failure, but then a lot of people might criticize that, saying I lack empathy of the world, of the men 10 miles away with only dirt to hug out there in the rain. Maybe I should then, be more correct to the audience: 2018 could be better.

Keyword: Love
Disclaimer: I do not understand what this word means.
I have been through failure on this path of the year, and I am sure I was not the only one who failed, and most certainly not the one who was hurt the most. This is not a romance story blog, but I want to say thank you to all the people who walked beside me this year. Not that I will not be here typing on the keyboard if you did not walk beside me, but things would just be a little harder. Just a little. laughs
Do not forget that southbound trains shall not go north.
I love you, Michelle.

Keyword: Memories
Keep them in a box, and never open it.

Keyword: Observation
Postulate insert a number of quantum mechanics states that observables are represented by hermitian operators. And guess what, hermitian operators have real eigenvalues! So is it that what is observable is real? It would makes sense, right?
There is always some new year resolution one make before the end of the year, and I think one of them should be me reminding myself to observe more of myself, and not others.

Keyword: Grades
Ok, we don’t talk about it here. At least not now.

Keyword: Blame
A lot of bad things can be tracked down to some source, and typically, it is what is to blame. But that does not mean it is what we end up blaming. I like to blame on things, or people, that is easily blamable (is this a word). Sleeping over a lab and end up getting a zero; well, obviously there is no one else to blame but myself, but I blame the sun, I blame the sky, and I blame the person beside me. It is always the hardest and last thing that we admit that it is us who fucked up.
So, second new year resolution, don’t fuck up.

Keyword: Growing up
When there are 2L of gin and 2L of red label in your apartment’s drawer, it is a sign of either I grew up, or I was really dumb. I’d like to believe it is the latter. Let’s call my life a PS4 game, and that I have missed a lot of achievements and unlockables early on in the game due to my incapability of reading instructions. Now, I have lost my chance to earn the platinum trophy. So…as any player would want, there should be a start new game option.
Well, sometimes I just have to be content with a bronze trophy.

Goodbye 2018,
Never see you again, because you have done your job!

She is saying, “please shave!”
